Pasta

Description:

Pasta is an Italian dish traditionally made with a combination of wheat flour, water and other ingredients such as vegetables, meats, herbs and spices. It is typically cooked by boiling the pasta in water until cooked through. The cooked pasta can be served hot or cold depending on the recipe, and it can come in a variety of shapes and sizes.

Ingredients

  • 250g of Pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 cups of vegatables of choices
  • 1 teaspooon dried oregano
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over high heat.
  2. Add desired amount of pasta to the boiling water.
  3. Stir often, for about 8 to 10 minutes, or until pasta is al dente.
  4. Drain the pasta in a colander and run cold water over it to stop the cooking process.
